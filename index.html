A Little About Us

We are a tight knit community who are passionate about cats. We want to ensure that every kitten finds a home where they are loved as much as they are with us. We take in cats who come from the streets and broken homes. We give them the love and care that they need until they are ready to start a new chapter in their life with a family who is everything that we are.
